Biography

Who Am I?

As a child of the 90s, I was brought up in parallel with the Internet. As I matured, the Internet matured. As I became an adult, the Internet became an adult. I watched the rapid explosion of the Web, the birth of ecommerce and the evolution of the PC from these large grey bulky machines to smooth little phones that fit in your pocket. Technology has always fascinated me

I was born in Germany, the fourth child and only son of an US Air Force captain and his wife. I lived in North Dakota for three years but I consider Maine my homestate, as I have lived there for more than 20 years. I am currently a member of the Air Force Reserves at Westover ARB, and a relationship manager at Barclaycard in Wilton ME.

I've always been a creator. It started with LEGOs, and I've always been building ever since. I write a lot and there's a lot of building in that, creating whole worlds out of text. Programming is also building, it's a ground up approach, much like with legos, starting with very small pieces and putting together something that works.
